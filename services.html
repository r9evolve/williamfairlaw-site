What We Offer

Closing & Disbursement Services

Our Legal and Disbursement Teams are here to deliver an excellent experience. Interested in learning more? Contact us: closings@wfairlaw.com

01

Real Estate Closings

We coordinate and manage the closing process for residential and commercial real estate transactions throughout Georgia. Our attorneys ensure all documents are properly prepared, executed, and recorded.

02

Disbursement Services

Our dedicated Funding Department handles the accurate and timely disbursement of closing funds to all appropriate parties. For disbursement inquiries, please call our office directly at (770) 676-6969.

03

Closing Attorney Representation

Georgia law requires a licensed attorney to oversee real estate closings. Our closing attorneys are available to guide buyers, sellers, and lenders through every step of the transaction process.

04

Title Examination & Review

Our team conducts thorough title examinations to identify any encumbrances, liens, or defects that may affect the transfer of property. We work diligently to resolve issues before closing.

05

Document Preparation

We prepare all required closing documents in compliance with Georgia state law and lender requirements, ensuring a smooth and legally sound transaction for all parties.
